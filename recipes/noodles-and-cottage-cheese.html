Noodles and Cottage Cheese

Description

An easy, Jewish and/or Eastern European comfort food. Easy to make in the microwave.

Ingredients

  • Noodles, cooked (any variety)
  • Cottage cheese

Steps

  1. Place noodles in microwave-safe bowl. Heat to desired temperature in microwave if not already hot (or about half if you also wish to heat cottage cheese).
  2. Mix cottage cheese into noodles in desired quantity.
  3. Optional: Heat more to also have warm cottage cheese.
