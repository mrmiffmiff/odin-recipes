Basic Cheese Quesadilla

Description

A very basic cheese quesadilla recipe for a quick snack. Simple, but delicious.

Ingredients

  • Shredded cheese
  • Tortilla

Steps

  1. Heat pan with personal standard regarding oil, Pam, or whatever.
  2. Place tortilla, open, into pan on medium heat.
  3. Cover tortilla with shredded cheese. Allow to melt.
  4. Lower heat a little and fold over one half of tortilla. Allow to brown slightly until crisp.
  5. Flip tortilla, brown other side slightly until crisp.
  6. Use best judgment to turn off heat and eat.

Alternative

You could instead just put a cheese-covered tortilla into the microwave. It's just as effective, though it won't be as crispy.
