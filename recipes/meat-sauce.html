Wonderful Meat Sauce Recipe

Description

This is a classic spaghetti sauce made using ground beef and manually-chopped vegetables. No premade spaghetti sauce.

This recipe is delicious and easy, and guaranteed to please everyone who eats it.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 28-ounce can diced tomatoes
  • 1 16-ounce can tomato sauce
  • 1 6-ounce can tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Prepare vegetables if not already done.
  2. Heat (medium-high) ground beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper in large saucepan. Cook and stir until meat browned and crumbly, vegetables tender. Approximately 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Drain grease.
  4. Stir in diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste.
  5. Add seasonings (oregano, basil, salt, pepper).
  6. Let simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
